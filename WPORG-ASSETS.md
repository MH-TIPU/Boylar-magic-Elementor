## WordPress.org assets checklist

These files live in your plugin’s WordPress.org SVN repo under `/assets/` (not inside the plugin ZIP).

### Icons (recommended)
- `icon-128x128.png`
- `icon-256x256.png`

### Banners (recommended)
- `banner-772x250.png`
- `banner-1544x500.png`

### Screenshots (match `readme.txt`)
If your `readme.txt` has 3 screenshots, upload:
- `screenshot-1.png`
- `screenshot-2.png`
- `screenshot-3.png`

### Tips
- Use PNG for crisp UI.
- Keep screenshots focused on: widget in panel, widget controls, generated section editable.
- Update the `== Screenshots ==` list in `readme.txt` if you change the count/order.
